Circuit Quantum Electrodynamics

Circuit quantum electrodynamics is the study of the interactions between light (photons or electromagnetic fields) and matter (electrons and atoms). The recent progress in creating and controlling quantum coherence in superconducting electrical circuits has been truly remarkable. The quantum electrodynamics of superconducting microwave circuits has been implemented ‘circuit QED’ as an analogy to cavity QED in quantum optics.
